## ğŸª‘ Placing Furniture

1. Open the furniture menu (icon in the HUD)
2. Browse available furniture items
3. Click an item to select it
4. Click on the grid to place it in your room
5. Items snap to the grid for clean placement

### Tips
- Furniture uses a grid-based placement system
- Some items may have collision, so place them thoughtfully
- Your room layout persists - changes are saved automatically
